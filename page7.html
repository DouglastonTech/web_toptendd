Number Six: Malaria
HOW TO CATCH IT:
Malaria is transmitted by mosquitoes and is both treatable and preventable. Most of these deaths occur in Africa where cures are unavailable or unaffordable. Malaria in Italian means "bad air."

PEOPLE KILLED PER YEAR:
Malaria kills 650,000-1.2 million per year, but studies show that the number of deaths is closer to 1.2 million people.

WHEN IT WAS DISCOVERED:
Malaria was discovered in 1880.
