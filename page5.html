Number Four: Lung Cancer
HOW TO CATCH IT:
Lung Cancer is cancer that starts in the lungs. Lung cancer is the leading cause of cancer deaths in the United States of America. People who smoke have the greatest risk of catching lung cancer. There is many ways to treat lung cancer but it has a low survival rate.

PEOPLE KILLED PER YEAR:
 Lung cancer kills 1.6 million people per year.

DISCOVERED:
 Lung cancer was discovered in 1961.
