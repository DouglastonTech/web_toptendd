Number Ten:Dengue Fever
HOW TO CATCH IT :
Dengue fever is spread by a mosquito carrying a tropical disease. It is also one of the fastest growing disease in the world. Dengue fever has no treatment or cure.

PEOPLE KILLED PER YEAR:
Dengue Fever kills about 25,000 people per year.

DISCOVERED:
Dengue Fever was discovered in 1943.
