Number Five: Pneumonia
HOW TO CATCH IT:
Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs. Pneumonia is the number one killer of children below the age five. Further Pneumonia can be contacted nearly anywhere. Luckily it is treatable and curable.

CHILDREN KILLED PER YEAR:
Pneumonia kills about 1.1 million children per year. Pneumonia was the most common cause of death in the 1900s.

DISCOVERED:
Pneumonia was discovered in 1875.
