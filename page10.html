Number Nine: Leishmaniasis
HOW TO CATCH IT:
Leishmaniasis is usually found in the poorest areas in the world with the biggest hotspots in Africa. Leishmaniasis is also growing in  Mediterranean areas. Leishmaniasis has no cure.

PEOPLE KILLED PER YEAR:
Leishmaniasis kills about 20,000 - 30,000 per year.

DISCOVERED:
Leishmaniasis was discovered in 1756.
