Number Two: AIDS
HOW TO CATCH IT:
Aids is also called HIV. AIDS is caught by body fluids. There is no cure for AIDS. AIDS weakens a persons ability to fight germs. Who you are and where in the world you live takes a big risk how HIV/ AIDS can affect your life.

PEOPLE KILLED PER YEAR:
AIDS kills about 1.6 million people per year, and infects 2.3 million per year. Also, in 1981 36 million people died around the globe.

DISCOVERED:
AIDS was discovered in 1980 and Dengue Fever was documented in 1981.
